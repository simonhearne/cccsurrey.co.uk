Are you self-isolating, worried about going out or need some help?

We are a group of people from the Surrey community who would like to help. We currently have community helpers in Bookham, Fetcham, Leatherhead, Ashtead, Cobham and surrounding areas.

Getting Help

Call 0800 3077 111 (freephone)

Tell us what help you need and we will share your requirements with a team of verified community helpers near you. Your helper will get in touch to organise support directly.
